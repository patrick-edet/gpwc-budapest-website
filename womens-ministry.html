We welcome you to our Women's Ministry

Dear Sister in Christ,

Grace and peace to you in the name of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ!

It is with joy in our hearts and deep gratitude to God that we warmly welcome you to the Women’s Ministry of the Global Presbyterian Worship Centre, Budapest, Hungary (GPWC). Whether you are a long-standing member or just beginning your journey with us, we want you to know that you are cherished, valued, and very much needed in this vibrant community of godly women.

Our Women’s Ministry is a sacred space where women of all ages and walks of life come together in sisterhood to grow in faith, serve with compassion, and shine the light of Christ wherever we go. Rooted in Scripture and guided by the Holy Spirit, our mission is to empower women to be bold in prayer, strong in faith, and active in ministry — in the home, the Church, and the wider world.

We believe that women play a vital role in God’s Kingdom. As mothers, daughters, sisters, wives, professionals, and leaders, we are called to reflect Christ’s love and truth in all we do. In this ministry, we seek to nurture spiritual maturity, encourage personal growth, and build a supportive fellowship where every woman is uplifted, inspired, and equipped to live out her God-given purpose.

Our vision is to see women:

  1. Growing deeper in their relationship with God through Bible study, prayer, and discipleship
  2. Strengthened in their roles as leaders, nurturers, and stewards of their homes and communities
  3. Engaged in meaningful service and mission — both within the Church and beyond
  4. Supporting one another through mentorship, fellowship, and genuine sisterhood
  5. Championing the values of love, humility, holiness, and unity as modeled by Christ

We gather regularly for worship, teaching, outreach, mentorship programs, and special events that address the unique spiritual, emotional, and practical needs of women. We believe in lifting one another up, praying together, and walking in unity as we fulfill our collective and individual callings.

No matter your season in life — whether you are single, married, widowed, a student, a professional, or a retiree — there is a place for you here. Your gifts, your experiences, your prayers, and your presence matter deeply to us. We encourage you to get involved, build meaningful relationships, and allow God to work through you in new and powerful ways.

As we journey together in faith and fellowship, we trust that God will continue to do great and mighty things through the women of this church. Together, we are building a legacy of faith, service, and love that will impact generations to come.

Once again, we welcome you with open arms and open hearts. May the Lord bless you richly and guide you as you take your place in this beautiful ministry of women committed to serving Christ and His Church.

With love and grace in Christ,


President, Women’s Ministry
Global Presbyterian Worship Centre, Budapest, Hungary
